About me

Gayathri G

As a dedicated consultant psychologist and founder of Prana - The Wellness Studio, I support individuals on their mental wellness journey. As a psychologist, I am dedicated to understanding the complexities of the human mind. I am deeply interested in studying advanced psychological theories and applying them in real life. I always explore different aspects of psychology, learning about how people think, behave, and manage their mental health. My academic journey included a challenging curriculum that fostered critical thinking and analytical skills. With extensive experience working in clinics with children, adults, and couples, I bring valuable practical insights to my work as a psychologist. Our team provides high - quality mental health services at a affordable way , fostering a safe and supportive environment for personal growth and resilience. Our mission is to empower individuals to navigate life's challenges with confidence and compassion.
